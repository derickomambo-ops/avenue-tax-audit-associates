Main taxes in Kenya

  • a) Corporation Tax — resident 30% / non-resident 37.5%
  • b) PAYE — deducted at source by employer
  • c) Withholding Tax — interest, dividends, fees, rent
  • d) VAT — introduced 1990, registered persons collect
  • e) Advance Tax — commercial vehicles (matatu)
  • f) Residential Rental Income (MRI) — 10% gross rent

Tax Advisory & Compliance

Tax is a compulsory contribution and a liability to an organization. In Kenya, tax is guided by the Tax Procedures Act No. 29. A taxpayer (with proper planning) can minimize on tax liability.

Section 73(3) - Estimated Assessments

Section 73(3) empowers the Commissioner to raise an Assessment where a taxpayer fails to submit a return, which Assessment is loosely referred to as "Estimated Assessments".

Once an Assessment is issued, it can only be vacated or amended upon receipt of a valid objection and submission of a return.

We undertake measures to ensure all tax assessment matters are resolved conclusively and effectively.
